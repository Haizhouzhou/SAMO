# Limitations and Ethics

SAMO is a research-code release candidate, not a clinical tool. It does not support diagnosis, screening readiness, deployment readiness, treatment decisions, or medical utility claims.

The labels are operational research labels. The target is reader-level, the available reader count may be small, and word-level observations are repeated evidence from the same readers. External generalization is not established in this release.

Privacy protection is central: no raw gaze files, real participant rows, participant-level predictions, or real word-level feature tables are redistributed. Users are responsible for following the access terms and privacy requirements of the data they obtain separately.
